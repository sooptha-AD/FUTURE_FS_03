Our Services

Beyond great coffee, we offer a range of services to make your experience special.

Dine-In Experience

Enjoy our cozy atmosphere with comfortable seating, free WiFi, and a relaxing ambiance perfect for work or leisure. Our cafe in Chikkamagaluru offers the perfect setting for friends and family.

Private Events

Host your special occasions in our dedicated event space. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gatherings, and coffee tasting sessions in the heart of Karnataka.

Catering Services

Bring the Velvet Bean experience to your office or event. Our catering menu features coffee, pastries, and light bites. Available across Chikkamagaluru district.

Gift Cards

Share the love with our gift cards. Available in any amount, perfect for coffee lovers in your life.
