Our Story

Crafting Moments Since 2015

Nestled in the misty hills of Chikkamagaluru, The Velvet Bean began as a small dream of two coffee enthusiasts. Today, we've grown into a beloved community space where art, culture, and exceptional coffee converge.

Our commitment to sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world, paired with our artisanal approach to roasting and brewing, ensures every cup is a masterpiece.

Being in the coffee capital of Karnataka, we take pride in offering our guests an authentic coffee experience that celebrates the rich heritage of Chikkamagaluru while bringing global coffee traditions to your cup.
